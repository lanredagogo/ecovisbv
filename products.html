Our Products

Discover our comprehensive range of high-quality energy products, carefully selected to meet the diverse needs of our customers. From aviation fuels to marine fuels and specialty products, we offer reliable and efficient solutions that empower your business.

Jet Fuels A1

Jet Fuel A1 is a high-quality aviation fuel designed to meet the stringent requirements of modern jet engines. It offers excellent performance, reliability, and low emissions, ensuring safe and efficient flight operations.

JP54

JP54 is another premium aviation fuel designed for specific types of jet engines. It offers similar benefits to Jet Fuel A1, including high performance, reliability, and reduced emissions.

Diesel EN590

Diesel EN590 is a high-quality diesel fuel designed for both marine and automotive applications. It offers excellent fuel economy, low emissions, and superior performance, making it a popular choice for various industries

Virgin Fuel Oil D6

Virgin Fuel Oil D6 is a high-quality fuel oil primarily used for marine applications. It offers good combustion properties, low sulfur content, and is a reliable fuel source for ships and vessels.

Mazut

Mazut is a heavy fuel oil commonly used for marine propulsion and industrial heating applications. It is known for its high energy content and relatively low cost, making it a popular choice for certain industries.

ESPO

ESPO (Eastern Siberian Pacific Oil) is a crude oil blend produced in Russia. It is a popular export grade known for its high sulfur content and relatively heavy density, making it suitable for refining into various petroleum products.
